About Ellie Pyo

Ellie Pyo is a Korean-American jazz violinist currently studying at Berklee College of Music

Born in Los Angeles, CA, she moved to Korea at the age of three and lived in five different cities over the next sixteen years.
During her childhood, Ellie was deeply interested in neuroscience, but while in high school, she discovered jazz and instantly fell in love with the music.
Two years later, that passion led her to begin her journey Berklee College of Music.
